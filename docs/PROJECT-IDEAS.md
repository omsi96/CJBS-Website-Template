# Project Ideas

Pick **one**. Each builds on the shared baseline (seeded students/staff + auth + design system).
The "first ticket" is a suggested smallest-useful slice to get your sprint moving. The example
**Elective Selections** slice already exists in the template as a reference — study it, then build
your own.

> Tip: the best projects have a tight loop — a student (or staff) *does something*, the system
> *remembers it*, and they can *see the result*. Start there.

---

### 1. Elective Selections  *(example included)*
Students browse the elective catalogue, select within limits, and see seat capacity / waitlists.
- **Personas:** MBA student, programme administrator.
- **First ticket:** "As a student, I can add an elective to my selections and see it on my dashboard."

### 2. Workshops Selection & Registration
A catalogue of short workshops students register for; capacity, schedule clashes, confirmations.
- **Personas:** student, workshop organiser.
- **First ticket:** "As a student, I can register for a workshop and see my registered list."

### 3. GCP — Global Consulting Projects
Match students to consulting projects: project listings, preferences, team formation, allocation.
- **Personas:** student, GCP coordinator, client.
- **First ticket:** "As a student, I can rank my top 3 project preferences."

### 4. Onboarding
A guided pre-arrival checklist: tasks, documents, milestones, progress tracking.
- **Personas:** incoming student, student-experience staff.
- **First ticket:** "As an incoming student, I can complete onboarding tasks and see my % progress."

### 5. Admissions / Scholarships
Application pipeline: submit an application, track status, scholarship eligibility, staff review.
- **Personas:** applicant, admissions officer.
- **First ticket:** "As an applicant, I can submit an application and track its status."

### 6. College Selection
Cambridge college matching: browse colleges, compare, express preferences, get allocated.
- **Personas:** incoming student, college liaison.
- **First ticket:** "As a student, I can shortlist colleges and submit a preference order."

### 7. Career Services / Recruitment
Job board + applications: roles, applications, interview tracking, coach notes.
- **Personas:** student, careers coach, recruiter.
- **First ticket:** "As a student, I can apply to a role and track my application stage."

### 8. MJBS
A community knowledge/marketplace hub for the MJBS community (listings, requests, profiles).
- **Personas:** community member, moderator.
- **First ticket:** "As a member, I can post a listing and browse others'."

### 9. Students / Staff Communication & Community
Announcements, channels, Q&A between students and staff; threads, read state, pinned posts.
- **Personas:** student, staff member.
- **First ticket:** "As a staff member, I can post an announcement that students see on their dashboard."

---

## Starting any of these
1. `/persona` to define who it's for.
2. `/prd` to write the requirements.
3. `/ticket` to slice the first user story.
4. Hand the ticket to Claude. Build. Verify against acceptance criteria. Repeat.

The seeded `Student` and `Staff` tables are your users for **every** idea — you never have to
create accounts from scratch.
